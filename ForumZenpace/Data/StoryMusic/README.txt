Thu vien nhac story dung chung

1. Giai nen goi nhac mien phi vao thu muc:
   ForumZenpace/wwwroot/library/story-music

2. Moi bai nhac co the duoc mo ta trong file:
   ForumZenpace/Data/StoryMusic/catalog.json

3. Dinh dang catalog:
[
  {
    "fileName": "ten-file.mp3",
    "title": "Ten bai",
    "artist": "Tac gia",
    "sortOrder": 10
  }
]

4. Neu khong khai bao catalog, he thong se tu dong quet tat ca file MP3/WAV/OGG/M4A trong thu muc library va hien ten dua tren file name.

5. Story da dang se chi luu track da chon, khong tao ban sao audio moi cho tung story.
